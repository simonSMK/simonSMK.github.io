about us

In the Dagoreti community, there are many children who have become orphans through the effects of alcoholism or AIDS. Most of these children are now under the care of their grandparents and some are in children's centers. Felista is the founder and director of Dagoreti Early Child Intervention Program (DECIP). The family Felista came from struggled to provide food and education, which caused her to not receive an adequate education. As a single mother, she struggled to provide for her children. Since she had no food at home, she started washing people's clothes and prayed that one day she would be able to feed and educate children in the community. It is has been her mission to provide food and clothing, education, access to medical care, a clean and healthy environment, and to protect the rights of orphans and vulnerable children. These are part of community development initiatives to assist drug addicts, dropouts, street children, and HIV-infected and affected individuals. Funding is an essential part of the organization in order to have enough food for all the children, to be able to provide more space in order to provide outreach programs, to be able to pay bills and cover basic needs, and to be able to maintain staff and board structures.

our story

The Dagoreti Early Child Education Intervention Programme is an orphan organization that was initiated in the year 1989 and duly registered in 2000. It started off as a feeding program alleviating poverty among street children and later became a home in 2007 after the post election violence as a rescue initiative of abandoned children at Nairobi show ground near Kibra. Only later a home was rented in waithatka with the help of Children Living With Aids Charity ( CWAC). In the fullness of time together with Lift the Children from Canada , the rented facility was extended for another three years . Henceforth with a loan later serviced by wings of support i.e. KLM a piece of land was purchased at Mutuini where the current home resides . Thereafter , Mama Biashara , a local CBO , Peace corps and friends of DECIP from the Netherlands community church , local and international business community including Hindu Community . Bob Collimore Foundation and several charitable initiatives assited in the construction of structures that exist up to this very day .
