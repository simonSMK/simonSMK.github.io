about us

The Dagoreti Early Child Education Intervention Programme is an orphan organization that was initiated in the year 1989 and duly registered in 2000. It started off as a feeding program alleviating poverty among street children and later became a home in 2007 after the post election violence as a rescue initiative of abandoned children at Nairobi show ground near Kibra. Only later a home was rented in waithatka with the help of Children Living With Aids Charity ( CWAC). In the fullness of time together with Lift the Children from Canada , the rented facility was extended for another three years . Henceforth with a loan later serviced by wings of support i.e. KLM a piece of land was purchased at Mutuini where the current home resides . Thereafter , Mama Biashara , a local CBO , Peace corps and friends of DECIP from the Netherlands community church , local and international business community including Hindu Community . Bob Collimore Foundation and several charitable initiatives assited in the construction of structures that exist up to this very day .

Our Goal

To improve the lives of needy children by providing adequate nutrition , food , shelter , clothing , education , training in life skills with moral and spiritual guidance.

Our vision

To be on the forefront in advocacy for care, support by responding to basic and fundamental Human rights of vulnerable Children in the community.

The Core Values of DECIP

Excellence:

We focus our energy and efforts efficiently and effectively to ensure greatest positive impact and output in our work while exhibiting the highest of professional ethics in the way we conduct our work.

Accountability:

We are committed to being good stewards of our resources and those of our stakeholders by exercising a thoughtful and cost effective use of the time, skills, and finances we have. We strive for quality and seek to maximize the opportunities to fulfill our mission.

Integrity, Honesty, and Openness:

We commit to integrating actively what we believe with what we do. We will do the right things for the right reason whether or not anyone will know.

Gender Equity:

We shall at all times strive to create an enabling environment free from discrimination, malice, and sabotage. We will promote equal opportunities for all, irrespective of tribe, religion, sex and any other form of discrimination.

Respect:

Our respect for one another compels us to fully value and affirm the dignity and uniqueness of each one of us. This respect is also the foundation for our good relationships and customer care with our clients, colleagues and other stakeholders.

Programs & Services

At Decip, we are committed to providing comprehensive programs and services that meet the diverse needs of children in our care. Our goal is to create a nurturing environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential. Here are some of the key programs and services we offer:

programs and services we offer:

Education Support:

Education is a priority at Decip. We offer tutoring, homework assistance, access to educational resources, and support to help children succeed academically. Our goal is to empower every child with the knowledge and skills they need for a brighter future.

Recreational Activities:

We believe in the importance of play and creativity. Our recreational activities include sports, arts and crafts, music lessons, cultural outings, and other engaging experiences that promote physical health, creativity, social skills, and self-expression.
