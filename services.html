Programs & Services

Programs & Services

At Decit, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive programs and services that cater to the diverse needs of children in our care. Our goal is to create a nurturing and supportive environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential. Explore our range of programs and services below:

Education Support:

Education is a priority at Decip. We offer tutoring, homework assistance, access to educational resources, and support to help children succeed academically. Our goal is to empower every child with the knowledge and skills they need for a brighter future.

Emotional Support Services:

Many of the children in our care have experienced trauma or adversity. Our dedicated team of counselors and therapists provides individual and group therapy sessions, emotional support, and coping strategies to help children heal, build resilience, and develop healthy coping mechanisms.

Healthcare Services:

We prioritize the health and well-being of our children by ensuring access to healthcare professionals, regular check-ups, vaccinations, and medical care as needed. Our healthcare services aim to promote physical wellness and address any health concerns promptly.

Nutritional Support:

Proper nutrition is essential for growing children. We provide nutritious meals, dietary guidance, and nutritional support to ensure that children have access to healthy and balanced nutrition, supporting their overall growth and development.

Recreational Activities:

We believe in the importance of play and creativity. Our recreational activities include sports, arts and crafts, music lessons, cultural outings, and other engaging experiences that promote physical health, creativity, social skills, and self-expression.

Family Reunification Support:

We work towards family reunification whenever possible. Our family reunification program includes counseling, parenting support, visitation programs, and transitional services to facilitate a smooth transition and ensure a stable and loving home environment for children.

Community Engagement:

We are actively engaged in our community through outreach programs, awareness campaigns, partnerships with local organizations, and volunteer opportunities. By raising awareness about children's needs and advocating for their rights, we strive to create a more supportive and inclusive society.
